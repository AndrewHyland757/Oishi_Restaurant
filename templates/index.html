The Restaurant

Don’t let the takeaway at the front fool you.

Oishī brings the bustling flavours and varieties of authentic Japanese food indoors with a restaurant experience that fuses an energetic ambience with killer tastes.

Our Specials

Some of our best loved dishes

From the pot

Tonkotsu Ramen

Classic noodles in an enriched soy sauce broth and roast pork belly & kikurage. Topped with spring onions, sesame seeds and & half a seasoned egg.

From the grill

Wagyu Steak

Our wagyu steak is from grass fed organic beef with an incredibly high level of fat marbling.

From the wok

Yasai Itame

A classic Japanese stir-fry available in a selection of meats and vegetables. A local favourite that never disappoints.

Meet The Chef

Yuri Yamamoto

Head Chef

Born in Osaka, Yukio has built a reputation as a pioneering and creative adventurer. Still only in his thirties, he continually surprises with his unique approach to Japanese cooking, which he describes as a fusion of street food and high cuisine.

His team of chefs and staff at Oishi create something new and exciting; bringing together seasons, aromas, temperature, textures and unique combinations to give you an experience you won't forget.
